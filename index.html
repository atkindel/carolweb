Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning

The Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning (CAROL) cultivates an interdisciplinary research community at Stanford focused on the unique affordances of online learning for research across the social sciences. The mission of CAROL is to steward data from online learning interactions and to develop standards for the ethical governance of research in digital learning environments.

About

CAROL manages data streams from instructional offerings on multiple digital platforms, curates these data for purposes of research, and maintains computational tools for their analysis by experts in a wide variety of fields.
