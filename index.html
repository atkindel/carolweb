Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning

The Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning (CAROL) stewards data from online learning environments to support responsible scientific inquiry. CAROL also articulates standards for the ethical governance of research in digital learning at Stanford and worldwide.

About

CAROL manages data from digital instruction conveyed on multiple platforms, curates these data for purposes of research, and maintains computational tools for their analysis by experts in a wide variety of fields.
