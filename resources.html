Resources for researchers

Stanford conveys digital learning opportunities on multiple online platforms, including Lagunita (an instance of the OpenEdX platform), Coursera, and NovoEd. Data collection infrastructure developed by CAROL converts platform exports into tabular formats that are amenable to analysis with many statistical software packages. More information on available data can be found here.

CAROL makes data from Stanford's online courses available to qualified Stanford investigators. We also share limited data collected from Stanford's public courses conveyed on Lagunita after June 14, 2014 with qualified investigators at other institutions.
