About the Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning

The growing ubiquity of instruction conveyed through digital media brings extraordinary opportunities for research across the human sciences. Data from digital learning environments has the potential to enable major advances in modeling human learning and can inform the design of better educational opportunities both on- and offline.

The Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning (CAROL) provides support for these endeavors. CAROL manages data streams from instructional offerings on multiple digital platforms, curates these data for purposes of research, and maintains computational tools for their analysis by experts in a wide variety of fields.

CAROL is housed within the Institute for Research in the Social Sciences and supports investigators throughout Stanford University. CAROL also makes online learner data available on a limited basis to qualified investigators worldwide.

Stanford is committed to maintaining the highest standards of ethical integrity in research in online learning environments.
