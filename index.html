Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning

The Center for Advanced Research through Online Learning (CAROL) stewards data from online learning environments to support scientific inquiry. CAROL also articulates standards for ethical governance of research through digital learning environments at Stanford and worldwide.

CAROL manages data from digital instruction conveyed on multiple platforms, curates these data for purposes of research, and maintains computational tools for their analysis by experts in many fields.
