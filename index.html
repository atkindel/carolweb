Center for Advancing Research through Online Learning

The Center for Advancing Research through Online Learning (CAROL) cultivates an interdisciplinary research community at Stanford focused on the unique affordances of online learning for research across the social sciences. CAROL is a hub for inquiry, connecting faculty and students interested in digital learning research with Stanford's unparalleled online learning resources and environments.

CAROL supports Stanford faculty and students conducting research in the University's digital learning environments; provides broad access to research datasets collected from learner interactions with Stanford's online course offerings; and catalyzes national conversations on the ethics and politics of scientific research in digital space.
