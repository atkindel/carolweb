Resources for researchers

Stanford conveys its digital learning offerings on three online platforms: Lagunita (an instance of the OpenEdX platform), Coursera, and NovoEd. Data collection infrastructure developed at CAROL converts platform exports into tabular formats that are amenable to analysis with common statistical software packages. In addition, CAROL works to produce high quality aggregate datasets for benchmarking purposes. More information on available data can be found at Stanford Datastage.

In addition to making digital learning data from all three platforms available to Stanford researchers, CAROL is able to share Lagunita data collected after June 14, 2014 with researchers at other institutions. We consider requests for data shares from researchers beyond Stanford solely for purposes of non-proprietary academic inquiry. Applications may be submitted at the VPTL Data Portal.
